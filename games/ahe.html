The remains of All Hallow's Eve - a 93/94 Event

Well, due to life getting in the way as-mentioned on the index page, All Hallow's Eve is dead and something else will become what it was. So what was it, anyways?

The Event

The Event is an Atlantic 93/94 "Old School" MTG event to occur sometime after November 13th, 2021. There is full prize support, The Event being funded out of my pocket for the fun of it, and everything is free to enter.

House Rules

There are a few house rules to go with The Event:

  • Proxies are allowed*
  • You may have as many copies of Plague Rats as you want in a deck
  • "Bands With Other" Lands may tap for one mana of their color identity

*You may only proxy up to two Power Nine cards in a deck.

Prizes

Prizes go as such:

  • 1st: 1x Alpha Creature Bond + First Choice of Playmat
  • 2nd: 2x Beta Creature Bond + Second Choice of Playmat
  • 3rd: 3x Unlimited Creature Bond + Third Choice of Playmat
  • Spiciest Deck: Surprise Card + Surprise Playmat
  • Best Theme: Surprise Card + Surprise Playmat

In addition to this list, all participants will be given one free "Event Card" for playing in The Event.
